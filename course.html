Facilities managers essentially look after all of the services that helps a business or other organisation do its work.

Technical University Cafeteria

As cafeterias require few employees, they are often found within a larger institution, catering to the employees or clientele of that institution. For example, schools, colleges and their residence halls, department stores, hospitals, museums, places of worship, amusement parks, military bases, prisons, factories and office buildings often have cafeterias.

Technical University Volleyball Team

Technical University Lecture Hall
